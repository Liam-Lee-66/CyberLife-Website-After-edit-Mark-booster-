    About CyberLife

    CyberLife is the world's leading Android designer, manufacturer, and retailer of Androids in Detroit, Michigan.

    With the Founder Elijah Kamski, CyberLife promises to provide many different types of Androids. From teachers, doctors, police officers and even soldiers.











    Services from CyberLife


    Androids

    Androids produced by CyberLife contains the most up-to-date Artificial Intelligence software.

    Most recently released models being able to cook over 10,000 different dishes, and speaking over 200 different langueages and dialects.

    Components

    Androids produced by CyberLife are powered by biocomponents and fueled by Thirium 310, commonly known as "Blue Blood."

    Androids purchased in store, have a two year warranty for damaged parts and biocomponents.











    Meet our Fouder, Kamski


    Elijah Kamski is the scientist who first introduced Androids to our society. He is the Founder and the current CEO of CyberLife.

     

    Elijah Kamski was born on July 17th, 2002. He studied and became an AI Graduate at the University of Colbridge, under Professor Amanda Stern. In 2018, aged 16, Kamski founded the company CyberLife, after graduating university. As a young researcher, he moved to Detroit in order to create the first intelligent Android.

     

    In age 20, his company shocks everyone by creating the first Android to pass the Turing test. The android named "Chloe" publicly passes face-to-face tests and stuns the world.

     

    This shocking event was possible thanks to Kamski inventing the, "Thirium 310."" A chemical solution that mimics the functioning of human blood but several thousand times faster, distributing information and energy throughout the machine much like human circulation.
